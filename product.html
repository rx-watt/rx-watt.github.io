A Full-Stack Solution

From component to network-level solutions.

1. Gateway

The fixed RF infrastructure creating coverage zones for battery-free and rechargeable devices.

  • Coverage-driven design.
  • Indoor or outdoor deployment options.
  • Supports the wider data and power architecture.

2. Wireless Power Harvester

A sensitive RF receiver based on our proven antenna design track record.

  • Built around efficient energy harvesting.
  • Supports compact, maintenance-free operation.
  • Fits multiple node and enclosure styles.
  • Bespoke design capability for specialist requirements.

3. Integrated Sensing Tags

Application-facing sensing endpoints designed for asset tagging, monitoring, and low-touch deployment.

  • Near-realtime reporting of temperature, humidity, motion, and more.
  • Suitable for compact or discreet placements.
  • Designed to feed data into existing workflows.
Technology Overview

Transmit power, harvest it locally, and collect meaningful sensor data.

1

License-Free Power Delivery Hub

Modular gateways which energize the tags in the 868/915 MHz band, collect Bluetooth sensor data, and provide multi-protocol/API cloud integration capability.

2

RF Power Harvesting

Sensitive and scalable power receivers, with an operation range up to 10-30 metres, depending on tag size and environment.

3

Integrated sensing

Propreitary patent-pending sensing network architecture which enables new use-cases from user activity monitoring to fault-detection.



RX Watt is playing an active role in standardizing RF wireless power delivery, working closely with the British Standards Institute (BSI) and the IEEE Standards committee.
